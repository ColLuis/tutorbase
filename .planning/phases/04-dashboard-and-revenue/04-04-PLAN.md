**Dashboard (http://localhost:3000/)**

1. Log in and confirm you land on the dashboard (not the old placeholder)
2. Verify the page heading says "Today" (if you have lessons today) or "Next up: [Day Date]" (if no lessons today)

4. Verify lessons are in chronological order
5. Tap a lesson row — confirm it navigates to `/schedule?week=...&lesson=...` and the lesson detail panel auto-opens
6. Check the 4 metric cards display correctly:

   - "Unpaid" — shows total of unpaid invoices and count
   - "This month" — shows revenue received this month
   - "This year" — shows revenue received this year
7. Check metric cards are 2x2 on mobile (narrow browser) and 4-across on desktop
8. Check the 3 quick action buttons at the bottom: "Add Lesson", "Create Invoice", "Add Student"
9. Tap each quick action button — confirm they navigate to the correct pages

**Revenue (http://localhost:3000/revenue)**

10. Navigate to the Revenue page via sidebar or bottom nav
11. Verify the "Revenue" heading and year navigation (< 2026 >) display
12. Click the left chevron (<) — confirm it shows the previous year's data
13. Confirm the right chevron (>) is hidden when viewing the current year
14. Check the Monthly Breakdown table has columns: Month, Lessons, Hours, Invoiced, Paid, Outstanding
15. Check all 12 months are shown (with dashes for months with no data)
16. Check the By Student table has columns: Student, Lessons, Invoiced, Paid, Outstanding
17. Tap column headers in the student table — confirm sorting works (ascending/descending toggle)
18. Verify the default sort is by Invoiced amount (highest first)
19. Check that sort direction icon (chevron) changes on tap
20. Confirm student rows are NOT tappable (no navigation on tap)
